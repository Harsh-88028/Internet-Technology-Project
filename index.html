WE ARE VALORANT

- About Us

Blend your style and experience on a global, competitive stage. You have 13 rounds to attack and defend your side using sharp gunplay and tactical abilities. And, with one life per-round, you'll need to think faster than your opponent if you want to survive.

Experience

Game Modes

We have permanent game modes Unrated, Competitive, Spike Rush, and Deathmatch and rotating game mode like Escalation, Replication, and Snowball Fight.

Act rank

The ranks are: Iron, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Ascendant, Immortal, and Radiant. With the exception of Radiant, each rank has three tiers.

Unique Gameplay

We have a variety of maps and have 20 agents to select from. Every agent has different abilities The game have a lot of cool skins for all different weapons.

VALORANT Challengers East: Surge Coming in 2023

Get ready for 2023! Today we're excited to share more details, and the full list of countries participating, around Eastern Europe's new VALORANT Challengers League, VALORANT Challengers East: Surge. Starting in 2023, players and teams from 23 countries across Central and Eastern Europe will compete in highly organised competition against each other - starting with open qualifiers and culminating in one team being crowned the inaugural champions.
